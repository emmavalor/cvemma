About Me

Hi ! My name is Emma Valor. I'm a Full Stack Developer and No-Code Developer.After spending a year and a half abroad to improve my English, I decided to turn to the world of the web, more precisely the website. At the end of 2020, I started to train myself in HTML and CSS on OpenClassroom. Bingo! I found my way. I start looking for a training, I want to improve my skills and learn more. In May 2021, I joined a 7-month Web and Mobile Web Developer training program with Simplon. HTML 5, CSS 3, Javascript, PHP, SQL databases, Agile Methods, we go through everything until I graduate on January 27th 2022.

After my diploma, I decide to continue to train, but this time in No-Code. Beginning of February 2022, I join a 6 weeks training divided in 2 parts: the first 3 weeks, modular No-Code with Contournement. I learn to use Airtable, Glide, Softr, Dorik. The last 3 weeks are focused on Bubble with Ottho. At ease with this great tool, I become a mentor with the Ottho team for their next Boot Camp.
